**Digital Campaign ROI Optimization & Conversion Analytics**

→ [View repo](https://github.com/YashINDataYRJ/Digital-Campaign-ROI-Optimization-Conversion-Analytics)

**Sashanka AgroTech — HR Recruitment System Analysis**  
Led a 69-point organizational diagnostic across 8 HR dimensions for an agro-tech firm. Identified 6 root causes behind 0 hires from 93 candidates. Delivered a transformation roadmap projecting Rs.80L+ ROI.

**Revenue Concentration & Demand Optimization Strategy**
